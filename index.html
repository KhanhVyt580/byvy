The Perfect Coffee Companion

The cookies have a cute shape like coffee beans, are very delicious, and have a lovely appearance. Follow the recipe below to learn how to make a great appetizer to sip with a cup of coffee every morning.

Coffee Bean Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken egg
  • 120g flour
  • 40g powdered sugar
  • 60g butter
  • 4g coffee
  • 10g cocoa

Instructions

  1. Dissolve coffee with hot water, stir well until the coffee dissolves completely. You can also make filter coffee, but pure filter coffee will make the cake taste more bitter.
